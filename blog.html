Testimony Devotional

Before I met God, I lived like a man wandering in a fog — priorities scrambled, purpose unclear. Small compromises became habits; my identity was tethered to achievements and approval. I chased the high of applause and the illusion of success, until one encounter changed everything.

Who I was

I was ambitious but restless. Example: I skipped family gatherings for opportunities that looked like advancement but felt hollow. My relationships suffered and my rhythms were chaotic.

The encounter

One evening, during a volunteer outreach, I felt an unshakeable peace and a conviction about truth, grace, and responsibility. It wasn't dramatic fireworks — just a deep, quiet repositioning of my heart.

What changed

  • Priorities became people-centered (relationships over résumé).
  • Success was redefined: obedience + fruitfulness, not applause.
  • Daily rhythms changed: prayer, Scripture, Sabbath rest.

If you are reading this, let it encourage you: one genuine encounter reorients the whole life. The result is not perfection — it's a faithful journey.
